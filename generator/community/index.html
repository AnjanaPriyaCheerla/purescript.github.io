Online

  • IRC, Slack and Gitter: Have questions or looking for help? Try the #purescript IRC channel on FreeNode, the #purescript channel on the Functional Programming Slack, or purescript/purescript on Gitter.
  • Mailing List: A Google Groups mailing list for discussing anything and everything related to PureScript.
  • Reddit (/r/purescript): A subreddit for community-submitted articles and links.
  • Twitter (@purescript): Follow along for news from the PureScript community.
  • GitHub Wiki: The wiki contains community-written articles about the language and its tools.
  • StackOverflow: Browse questions tagged with PureScript or ask one of your own.
  • Remote PureScript Meetups (Talks and Hacks): Organised out of the Bay Area PureScript Meetup account, these are starting tentatively on the second Saturday of the month.

In Real Life

  • Bay Area (California) PureScript Meetup
  • PureScript Conf 2016: A side-event co-located with LambdaConf 2016 this year in Boulder, Colorado.
